About Us

The Wildcats AI & Cloud Computing Club at Northwest Vista College empowers students to turn ideas into reality. Dive into cloud computing, experiment with AI, and collaborate on hands-on projects that challenge you to innovate and build real-world solutions. Whether you're coding your first program or developing the next big AI tool, our club gives you the skills, experience, and creativity to learn, grow, and prepare for a future in technology. Come to create and level up your tech game with the Wildcats.
